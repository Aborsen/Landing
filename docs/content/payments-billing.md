## Where billing lives

Everything billing-related is on **Settings → Billing**, visible to workspace Admins. From there you can view the current plan, the next renewal date, the running token balance, every past invoice, and the payment method on file. Members of the workspace see none of this — billing is intentionally kept private to Admins.

## Payment methods

Insightis accepts every major credit and debit card processed through Stripe. Business and Enterprise plans can additionally pay by ACH transfer or wire — switch to invoice-based billing from **Settings → Billing → Payment method**. Card details are tokenized by Stripe and never stored on Insightis servers; we hold only the last four digits and the brand so you can recognize the card on file.

## Invoices and receipts

Every charge generates a downloadable PDF invoice with your workspace name, billing address, and a per-line breakdown of seats and token packs. Invoices are emailed to the billing email address on the day they are issued, and the full history stays available indefinitely under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. If your finance team needs a different billing address or a tax ID on the invoice, update those fields and the next invoice will reflect the change.

## Buying more tokens

If your monthly subscription tokens are exhausted before the next renewal, buy a one-time token pack from **Settings → Billing → Tokens**. These purchases are one-time charges, never recurring, and the tokens are usable immediately. One-time tokens do not expire — anything you do not use stays on your balance until you need it, and Insightis only draws on them once your monthly allowance is spent. Pro and Enterprise workspaces can enable **Auto top-up** to add a token pack automatically whenever the balance falls below a threshold, so AI Chat never pauses unexpectedly.

## Changing or cancelling a plan

Plan upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date, so you keep the higher-tier features until the period you already paid for ends. To cancel entirely, click **Cancel subscription** at the bottom of the Billing page; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current period and your workspace switches to the Free plan.

## Help with billing

If something looks wrong on an invoice — duplicate charge, unexpected amount, refund request — email billing@insightis.ai with the invoice number and a brief description. Most billing issues are resolved within one business day.
