## Your profile

Open **Settings → Profile** to update your name, display photo, time zone, and default region. The time zone setting is important: every chart, schedule, and saved report renders in your local time, and getting it right keeps "last week" meaning the same thing for everyone on the team.

## Email and password

Change your sign-in email from **Settings → Account**. You will receive a verification link at the new address; the change takes effect once you click it. Passwords can be reset at any time from the same page; if your workspace enforces single sign-on, password fields are hidden and your identity provider manages the credential.

## Managing teammates

Workspace Admins can invite, remove, and re-assign roles from **Settings → Team**. Three roles are available out of the box:

- **Member** — can ask questions in AI Chat, view shared reports, and use certified metrics.
- **Editor** — everything Members can do, plus build reports and propose metric definitions.
- **Admin** — full control over connectors, certifications, billing, and team membership.

For finer-grained access, attach row-level and column-level rules directly to a connector — for example, "this teammate can see Stripe customer rows tagged with region = EU".

## Workspace defaults

Admins can set workspace-wide defaults from **Settings → Workspace**: the default region for new reports, the default time zone for schedules, the brand color used on shared report pages, and the company logo that shows up on exported PDFs. These defaults can be overridden per report or per teammate, but they keep new content consistent without anyone having to think about it.

## Leaving or deleting an account

You can leave a workspace at any time from **Settings → Account → Leave workspace**. Reports you authored and metrics you certified stay in the workspace under an "Original author left" attribution. If you want to fully delete your Insightis account — including all workspaces where you are the sole owner — submit a request from **Settings → Account → Delete account** and we will process it within the deletion windows described in the Data Storage page.
