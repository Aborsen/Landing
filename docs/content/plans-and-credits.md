## The three plans

Insightis is offered in three tiers, all designed so a team can grow without changing tools:

- **Free** — for individuals exploring the product. One workspace, the AI Chat experience, and a starter pack of credits each month.
- **Starter** — for small teams who need to share answers and define a few metrics together. Currently $9.99 per seat per month with a 50% launch discount applied.
- **Pro** — for teams running on Insightis day to day. Includes the full Semantic Layer, unlimited connectors, scheduled reports, and a larger monthly credit pool. Currently $19.99 per seat per month with the same launch discount.

The latest plan details and feature comparison are on the Pricing page at insightis.ai/pricing.

## What credits are

Every answer in AI Chat consumes a small number of credits. Credits cover the cost of running the AI models behind your questions and the queries against your connected sources. Most questions consume a single credit; longer multi-step analyses, large export operations, or scheduled reports may consume more. The Workspace settings page shows a live counter so you can see exactly where your credits have gone.

## How credits refill

Each plan comes with a monthly credit pool that resets on your billing date. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month, but you can purchase additional credit packs at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Pro and Enterprise workspaces can also enable auto-top-up so AI Chat never pauses mid-conversation.

## Upgrading, downgrading, and cancelling

Plan changes take effect immediately on the same workspace — no data loss, no migration. Downgrading keeps your historic reports and metric definitions intact, but features that are not in the lower plan stop working at the next billing cycle. You can cancel any time from Settings → Billing; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

## Need something different?

If your team needs custom credit volume, dedicated infrastructure, or enterprise terms (SSO, audit log export, custom data residency), reach out to sales@insightis.ai for an Enterprise contract.
