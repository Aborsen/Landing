## What tokens are

Tokens are the unit of AI usage inside Insightis. Every AI Chat query and every metric the AI proposes consumes tokens — they cover the cost of running the models behind your questions and the queries against your connected sources. Most questions use a small number of tokens; longer multi-step analyses use more.

A live counter in the sidebar shows your current balance at all times, and turns amber and then red as the balance runs low, so you are never surprised mid-analysis. There are two kinds of tokens, and Insightis draws on them in a simple order.

## Subscription tokens

Every plan includes a monthly allowance of subscription tokens. These are the tokens you use day to day, and they work on a monthly cycle:

- Your full allowance is available at the start of each billing month.
- Whatever is left at the end of the month is burned — subscription tokens do **not** roll over into the next month.
- At the start of the next month, your allowance is refilled back to the plan amount.

Because they reset every month, subscription tokens are sized to your team's expected monthly usage. If you consistently run out before the month ends, that is the signal to move to a larger plan.

## One-time token packs

If you run out of your monthly subscription tokens, you can buy a one-time token pack at any time — no need to wait for the next billing cycle or upgrade your plan. One-time tokens behave differently from subscription tokens in one important way:

- They **do not expire**. A pack lasts until you have used it, however many months that takes.
- They are consumed only after your monthly subscription tokens are used up, so you always spend your included allowance first.

This makes one-time packs a flexible top-up for busy months or one-off projects: buy what you need, and anything you do not use stays on your balance for later.

## How the two work together

Put simply: subscription tokens are your renewing monthly budget, and one-time packs are a permanent reserve.

- Insightis spends your **subscription tokens first**.
- When those run out, it falls back to any **one-time tokens** on your balance.
- At month end, unused subscription tokens are burned and the allowance refills; unused one-time tokens carry over untouched.

You can **Buy more tokens** or **Upgrade plan** at any time from **Settings → Billing**.

## The plans

Insightis is offered as a Free plan plus paid tiers that scale with your team — larger token allowances, more storage, and more connected data sources as you grow. The Free plan lets you evaluate the product end to end before you pay.

The current plans, prices, and full feature comparison are always on the Pricing page at insightis.ai/pricing.

## Upgrading, downgrading, and cancelling

Plan changes take effect immediately on the same workspace — no data loss, no migration. Downgrading keeps your historic metric definitions and connected sources intact, but features that are not in the lower plan stop working at the next billing cycle. You can cancel any time from Settings → Billing; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
