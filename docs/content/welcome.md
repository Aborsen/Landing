## Why Insightis exists

Most analytics tools assume you can write SQL, build a dashboard, or wait three days for someone in the data team to do it for you. Insightis takes a different approach: connect your data once, define your metrics once, and from then on everyone in the company can simply ask. The answers come back with a chart, a number, and a written explanation in seconds.

## Who Insightis is for

Insightis is built for the people who need answers fast — founders, revenue and marketing leaders, product managers, operations and finance teams — and for the data teams who want to govern those answers without becoming a bottleneck. If you have ever copy-pasted a number from a CSV into a slide deck, Insightis is for you.
