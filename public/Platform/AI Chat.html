Frequently asked questions

What is Insightis AI Chat?

Insightis AI Chat is a conversational analytics interface from Devart that lets anyone query your business data in plain English. It connects to your warehouse and SaaS tools, applies your Semantic Layer, and returns precise answers with charts — no SQL, no dashboards, no analyst queue.

How is Insightis different from ChatGPT or generic AI?

Generic AI answers from public internet data, returning industry averages instead of your numbers. Insightis queries your live data via a governed Semantic Layer — so "what is our churn?" returns your 2.1%, not a 3–8% SaaS benchmark. Three times more accurate on business questions.

Do I need SQL to use Insightis?

No. Type questions the way you'd ask a colleague — Insightis translates them into queries against your warehouse, runs them, and returns an answer with a visualization. Analysts can still drop into SQL when needed, but end users never see it.
